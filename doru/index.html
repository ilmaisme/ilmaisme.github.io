Backstory

Welcome to Doru Planet. Where in this universe, you'll meet two types of Doru clans: Freado and Zydo. Freado has a mission to educate all the Doru Planet community. Aside from his effort to build each other, he also loves to try new things. His style is also varied and very freaky but cool.

And you'll meet Zydo (Lazy Doru), the opposite of Freado. He has a free spirit and wants to run free. He despises education, but he wants more freedom.

So snatch them up and proudly claim your favorite Doru and let's grow our adventure together.

Types of Doru

Zydo

Loves to build his community with literacy, passion, and love to try new things. He is also inspiring in the community and very transformative in his style.

Freado

Rebellious and free-spirited character. He's the naughtiest Dorucat on Planet Doru and is also too lazy to read and learn. Zydo refuses all literacy-related things.
