Our Great Stories

Established in 1970, PT Gramedia Asri Media or Gramedia Store is one of the strategic business units under Kompas Gramedia Group which is focused on the retail business with books and stationery as its main products.

This is related to the mission to be involved in the efforts to educate and enlighten the nation’s life through the distributionof information and knowledge. Until now, Gramedia has 120 stores spread from Sabang to Merauke, in 33 provinces and 54 cities throughout Indonesia.

In 2022, Gramedia will celebrate its 52nd anniversary. The journey to the age of 52 is inseparable from the support and involvement of the Indonesian people, even amid the unpredictable ups and downs situation until today.

Nevertheless, we want to invite people to keep celebrating ideas, inspiring each other, and collaborating in the creative ecosystem of literacy. In supporting this initiative, Gramedia brings Online Virtual Festival 2022 through #KolaborasiTanpaBatas by providing a space contained with various inspiring content and activities.

Everything that is big and significant starts small and grows over time. That is how Gramedia started. The long history of success began from a single bookstore in 1970. Built at Jl. Gajah Mada, it was a small store of only 252 square feet. But P.K. Ojong, Gramedia’s founder, had a big dream. He dreamed of helping as many Indonesians as possible to learn and thrive by providing books, stationery and everything essential to acquire knowledge.
