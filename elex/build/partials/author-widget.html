Puti Karina (Puty) Puar

Puty Puar is an illustrator, content creator, book author, and community leader. She is also known as 'byputy' on social media for her heartwarming artworks, with a 100,000+ audience across platforms, and has worked with various brands and organizations. Puty founded and manages ‘Buibu Baca Buku’, a non-profit community aiming to empower Indonesian women, especially mothers, through literacy. She believes in the collective power of women to create, not only massive impacts but also long-lasting ones.
